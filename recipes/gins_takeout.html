Gin's Takeout Stirfry

From the official Sanji recipe book (that doesn't use seafood for it)

Ingredients for 2

  • 360 grams (g) of coocked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g of corned beef
  • 50g of onion
  • 4 Paris mushroom
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 pinch of black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon of Soja sauce
  • a few pinches of spring onion.
  • vegetable oil

Preparation :

Finely chop the onion and the sping onion, remove the base of the mushroom stalk, and cut them into fine slices. Grease the pan with the oil, brown the mushrooms alongside half the onion, add the corned-beef and brown it. Put everything together on the side of the pan, grease the space remaining with the oil, pour the beaten eggs into it. Add the coocked rice before the eggs harden. Saut√© quickly and mix everything together. Add the salt, the pepper, and the remaining onions. Pour the soja sauce starting from the side of the pan and then mix rapidly. Transfer everything into another dish and sprinkle with the sping onion.
